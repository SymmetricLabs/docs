## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your API Key">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Your First Call">
    Test the connection with the health check endpoint
  </Step>
  <Step title="List Your Workflows">
    Discover available workflows in your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Workflow">
    Execute your first automated workflow
  </Step>
</Steps>

  All API endpoints require authentication using Bearer tokens. Rate limits apply to ensure fair usage across all users.
</Note>
